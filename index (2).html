Deities Covens Kingdoms

The Deities

Bilanism is centered around the divine entity Bilan, split into two opposing yet complementary forces:

  • Iyera: The deity of light, creation, and nurturing aspects of existence.
  • Salider: The deity of shadow, destruction, and chaos.

Other minor deities represent elements, truth, lies, healing, and curses.

The Covens

Covens are groups associated with specific deities and elements. Each has its unique focus:

  • Earth Coven: Silver Grove (Gaia) / Dark Thorn (Terragon).
  • Water Coven: Crystal Waters (Aquilia) / Tempest's Fury (Vortrex).
  • Fire Coven: Luminous Flames (Zephyr) / Blazing Inferno (Ignatius).
  • Air Coven: Celestial Winds (Aria) / Crimson Skies (Aerion).
  • Truth/Lies Coven: Radiant Sanctum (Lysara) / Shadowed Veil (Nethra).
  • Healing/Curses Coven: Luminous Veil (Aelora) / Umbral Nexus (Moros).

The Kingdoms of Eldoria

Eldoria consists of twelve kingdoms, each representing a deity. For example:

  • Vyreth: A bustling kingdom embodying Nethra. Known for its towering dark buildings, neon lights, and shifting political powers.
